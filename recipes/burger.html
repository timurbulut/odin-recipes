Simple Burger

I love me some burgers. I also love not really cooking if I don't feel like it, here's how to combine the two

You will need:

  • Ground beef
  • Half an onion, sliced
  • American cheese
  • A lot of sauce
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Herbs and spices

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the meat, breadcrumbs, herbs and spices and some ketchup.
  2. Pound that all together with an egg as well, why not. Anyway once that's done shape them into patties.
  3. Grill those patties in a George Foreman registered trademark grill. Otherwise fry them on a non-George Foreman regular frying pan.
  4. Toast your buns, put butter on them. Burgers are already unhealthy why skimp on additional flavour
  5. put the patties in the buns then you're done. Slather a bunch of sauce on it because it will probably not detract from any of the taste
