Beer Braised Bratwurst Dogs

Frankfurters are only really good when you're a kid. Once you grow up those horribly processed, pink slime tubes just taste like sweat. I'll just take a regularly processed tube of meat, thanks.

You will need:

  • Frying pan with lid
  • 6 Bratwurst sausages
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Good lager or beer

Directions:

  1. Fry brats in some oil for about 5 mins, turning frequently. We just want a little browning.
  2. Take those brats out, toss in the onion and fry till golden and translucent.
  3. Add those brats right back in, sprinkling in a bit of salt and pepper.
  4. Pour lager over, enough to halfway submerge the brats. Turn heat down to a gentle simmer.
  5. Simmer for about 12 minutes, then they should be done. If the sausages split, who cares? It's only you eating it
  6. Put those sausages in their buns, layer the onion on top, add any desired sauces.
  7. Don't forget to down that beer. Don't let it go to waste
